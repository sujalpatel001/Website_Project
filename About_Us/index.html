Brief Introduction

We are manufacturers of all types of cable-making machinery, having worked in the cable industry for at least 15 years. Our team comprises young and dynamic professionals with extensive experience in the cable industry. Collectively, the top levels of our organization boast at least 50 years of cumulative experience. Our leadership team has worked with various types of cables such as Power Cables, Control Cables, Rubber Cables, Jelly Filled Telephone Cables, Optical Fiber Cables, and more. The head of our organization also has experience working as a Maintenance and Project Manager both in India and abroad within the cable industry. Currently, we operate two production units in Umbergaon, Gujarat.
We have already modified our processes and continue to improve using the latest technology available in the industry. We take pride in our management and staff for achieving and maintaining this reputation in the field of cable-making machinery manufacturing.


Present Activity

Jyoti Engineering supplies machines that are manufactured at its two units situated in Umbergaon, Gujarat. The types of machines vary depending on the requirements and applications of the customers.
The company is a recognized and dependable supplier of high-quality machines approved by leading cable manufacturers. We enjoy the patronage of high-profile cable manufacturers, thus enabling our organization to receive repeated orders and improve competency and commitment towards existing customers.


The Work Force

This organization is headed and managed by experienced technocrats in a very professional manner. The rich and wide experience of the team involved in the field of the cable industry makes our work very customer-friendly.
The company management has always had the policy to update the range of products as well as implement modern techniques both in production methods and management.
The organization has always tried to understand the needs of the industry and developed its organization to meet the challenge of the changing environment.
The management has always considered customer satisfaction of the utmost importance and has built this philosophy of the company to the very grassroots level because we understand the philosophy that ‘A satisfied customer is the best advertisement for any company’. The management of the organization believes that any commitment given by the company must be fulfilled at all costs.


Quality Control

At Jyoti Engineering, we have adopted very stringent quality control measures to ensure uniform acceptance quality of the products and their application at all times. We also understand that quality requirements differ with each application. Recognizing this fact has enabled us to establish different types of norms to be followed depending on the requirements of the clients, consultants, the application, and the accuracies needed.


After Sales Service

We have a very expert and dynamic team of after sales maintenance. The people engaged in after sales services are having rich experience in cable industry and are capable to modify and improve the machine for the extra requirement of the customer.
We never hesitate to depute our service engineers when the client desires so and ensure that the customers remain satisfied always. Due to the critically of heavy and high speed rotational machines, we always try to make the design simple and maneuverable so that the down time decreases and the overall outputs are increased.
